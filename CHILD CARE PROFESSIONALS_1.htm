Child Care Professionals

the vision

from the beginning

Evidence shows that the developmental needs of a child (such as academics, social, emotional and physical) require professionals who have earned credentials in their field. Programs for Parents’ trainers help early childhood professionals achieve certification that acknowledges their skills in early childhood development. In addition, our trainers provide ongoing training to refresh and expand their knowledge in this evolving area. Training is available to meet the needs of staff at all levels, from entry level to executive. PfP trainers have all met the meet all Approved Trainer qualifications required for the Professional Impact New Jersey Registry by the NJ Workforce Registry. www.njccis.com.

To see the full range of training, information and services we provide to child care professionals, please browse our expandable navigation menu above.
